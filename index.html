Real-time tracking: Monitor your assets in motion, 24/7. Our GPS tracking solutions empower you to track the precise location, origin, and destination of your vehicles or assets.

Route Control and optimization: Optimize operations and save time and resources. We enable you to control and monitor your vehicle's routes, helping you make informed decisions about your assets' usage and efficiency.

Driver behavior monitoring: Enhance safety with driver behavior insights.

Asset maintenance alerts: Stay on top of maintenance needs. Our GPS system provides timely reminders for your vehicle's service intervals, ensuring optimal performance.

Comprehensive reporting: Access detailed reports for informed decisions. Receive detailed reports on actions, such as overspeeding and late parking, to enhance asset management.

Cost savings: Reduce fuel and maintenance costs with smart asset management.

Comprehensive GPS Installation Services: We offer cost-effective GPS installation for a wide range of assets, including televisions, laptops, refrigerators, music systems, motorcycles, automobiles, Bajaj, tractors, and various other assets.

Remote Control: Manage your vehicles and assets remotely. You can disable your vehicle or motorcycle via a user-friendly mobile application or computer interface.

Business Efficiency: Manage your business more effectively by monitoring fuel consumption and enhancing resource allocation.

Security Features: Enjoy complete security control by scheduling specific times for your vehicle to remain inactive, safeguarding your assets during non-operational hours.

Regulatory Compliance: Stay compliant and avoid penalties with reminders for Sumatra fee payments, driver's license renewals, and vehicle insurance renewals.

Unauthorized Usage Prevention: Prevent unauthorized usage by configuring your assets to remain within specific boundaries. In the event of any movement, the system will send SMS and email notifications.

Compliance Control: Ensure your assets are used in adherence to company policies and regulations.
